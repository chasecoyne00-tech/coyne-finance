Capabilities

Finance built around the asset, the borrower and the opportunity.

Coyne Finance arranges asset-backed and commercial lending across a broad range of asset types and borrower profiles. Each transaction is structured to suit lender appetite and the commercial outcome — not forced through a single product.

01

Vehicle Finance

Funding for commercial and private vehicles, structured to suit business or personal use, with terms aligned to the asset and the borrower’s profile.

02

Truck & Transport Finance

Finance for prime movers, trailers and transport fleets, structured for owner-operators and established transport businesses alike.

03

Equipment Finance

Asset-backed funding for the equipment that runs your business, from a single unit to a full fit-out.

04

Machinery Finance

Finance for plant and machinery across construction, manufacturing and industry, positioned to lenders who understand the asset class.

05

Yellow Goods Finance

Funding for earthmoving and heavy plant such as excavators, loaders and dozers, for civil, construction and resource operators.

06

Caravan & Leisure Asset Finance

Structured finance for caravans and leisure assets, arranged with the same considered approach as commercial lending.

07

Commercial Vehicle Finance

Finance for utes, vans and light commercial fleets, structured around business use and cash flow.

08

Low-Doc Business Finance

Flexible low-doc and alt-doc structures for self-employed and ABN-holding borrowers whose income documentation is more complex than a payslip.

09

Startup & ABN Finance

Funding pathways for newer businesses and ABN holders where the asset and the structure support the application.

10

Private & Dealer Sale Finance

Whether you are buying through a dealer or privately, the transaction is structured and positioned the same considered way.

11

Refinance & Asset Release

Restructure existing facilities or release equity from assets you already own to fund growth.

Credit Guide

Who we are, our role in arranging finance, and what you can expect from us.

Last updated: June 2026

About this Credit Guide

This Credit Guide provides information about Coyne Financial Pty Ltd and the credit assistance we provide. It is designed to help you decide whether to use our services. Please read it together with any other documents we give you.

About Coyne Finance

Coyne Financial Pty Ltd is a Credit Representative (Credit Representative Number [XXXXXX]) of AFAS Group Pty Ltd, Australian Credit Licence 414426. We are authorised to provide credit assistance in relation to consumer and, where applicable, commercial asset and equipment finance.

The credit assistance we provide

We assist you to identify and apply for credit that is appropriate to your needs and circumstances. This includes assessing your requirements and objectives, helping you select a suitable lender and product, and arranging the application through to settlement.

Our lenders

We have access to a panel of lenders and finance providers. A list of the lenders we work with is available on request.

Suitability and responsible lending

Where the law requires it, before we provide credit assistance for a consumer credit product we will make reasonable inquiries about your requirements, objectives and financial situation, take reasonable steps to verify that information, and assess whether the credit contract is not unsuitable for you. You may request a copy of any such assessment.

Fees, charges and commissions

Lenders may pay us a commission for arranging finance, and in some cases we may charge a fee for our services. Where a fee applies, we will disclose the amount (or the method of calculating it) before you proceed. You will receive details of any commissions and fees in your finance documentation.

If something goes wrong

If you have a concern or complaint, please contact us first. Details of how to make a complaint, and your right to access free external dispute resolution, are set out on our Complaints & Dispute Resolution page.

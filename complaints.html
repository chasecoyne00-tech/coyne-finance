Complaints & Dispute Resolution

If something isn’t right, we want to hear about it — and to put it right.

Our commitment

Coyne Financial Pty Ltd is committed to dealing with complaints fairly, promptly and respectfully. If you are not satisfied with any part of our service, please let us know so we can work to resolve it.

How to make a complaint

You can contact us directly:

  • Email: Chase@coynefinance.com
  • Phone: 0473 896 509
  • Post: [Postal address]

Please include your name, contact details and a description of your concern, along with any relevant information, so we can investigate properly.

How we will handle it

We will acknowledge your complaint promptly and work to resolve it within the timeframes required under applicable industry standards and our licensee’s process. We will keep you informed and let you know the outcome and the reasons for our decision.

If we can’t resolve it together

If your complaint is not resolved to your satisfaction, you have the right to access free, independent external dispute resolution. Coyne Finance arranges credit as a Credit Representative of AFAS Group Pty Ltd (Australian Credit Licence 414426), which is a member of the Australian Financial Complaints Authority (AFCA).

Australian Financial Complaints Authority (AFCA)
Website: afca.org.au
Email: info@afca.org.au
Phone: 1800 931 678 (free call)
Post: GPO Box 3, Melbourne VIC 3001

External dispute resolution is a free service available to you if you are not satisfied with how your complaint has been handled.
